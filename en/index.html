Welcome from Finec MGIMO Dean#

At Finec MGIMO we teach economics, management and business IT at undergraduate and masters levels.

We are located at MGIMO Odintsovo campus, which is becoming our university hub for technology initiatives, including broad use of machine learning in social sciences, study of financial sector innovations, event and media, and seamless digitally-enabled international trade.

We run this website from a Github repo, check it out for more curriculum documentation, code and data projects.

Please reach us if you have any questions about student affairs (admissions, transfers or academic exchanges), joint research or other academic cooperation initiatives.
